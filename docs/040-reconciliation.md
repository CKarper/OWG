# Reconciliation

## Purpose

OWG exists to reconcile identity across disparate sources.

Examples include:

- ISBN
- DOI
- Wikidata
- Open Library
- Publisher identifiers
- Institutional identifiers

## Sources and Assertions

Sources provide assertions.

OWG records provenance and mappings.

## Resolution

Resolution is a service layered on top of identity.

Consumers may choose different resolver policies.

Identity remains stable regardless of resolution policy.

## Transparency

Consumers should be able to inspect provenance, sources, and reconciliation history.
